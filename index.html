PuttPilot

Your AR mini-golf aim caddie

  1. Frame the hole — point your phone down the lane.
  2. Tap the 4 corners of the playing surface.
  3. Tap the ball, then tap the hole.
  4. PuttPilot draws the exact line — including bank shots.

100% on your device. No account, no upload, no tracking. Your camera never leaves your phone.

⚠

Camera needed

PuttPilot needs your camera to see the hole.

  • Tap Retry and choose Allow.
  • On iPhone, open this link in Safari (not an in-app browser).
  • Check Settings ▸ Safari ▸ Camera is set to Allow.

How to use

  • Hold steady. The overlay is locked to where you tapped. If you move the phone a lot, tap Reset and recalibrate (or Freeze the frame first).
  • Corners: tap the 4 corners of the flat playing surface in any order.
  • Ball & hole: tap the ball, then the cup.
  • Bank shots: if the straight line is blocked, PuttPilot finds the wall bounce. Tap Next route to see alternates.
  • Slope: tap Slope, then drag in the downhill direction (longer drag = steeper). The line curves accordingly.
  • + Wall: add an interior wall/obstacle edge by tapping its two ends.
  • Power: the slider shows how hard to hit. Green = it drops. Match the suggested mark.

PuttPilot is a visual guide, not a guarantee — real turf has nap and slope it can't see. Use it to read the line, then trust your stroke.
